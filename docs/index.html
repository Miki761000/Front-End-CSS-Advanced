  • Europe

    Paris, France

    Art is highly regarded in France and France is known for the art collections in museums in their country. The most famous art gallery in the world, The Louvre, is located in Paris, France. It is home to the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo. Many fashion houses are located in Paris.

  • America

    San Francisko, USA

    San Francisco, in northern California, is a hilly city on the tip of a peninsula surrounded by the Pacific Ocean and San Francisco Bay. It's known for its year-round fog, iconic Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars and colorful Victorian houses. In the bay sits Alcatraz Island.
